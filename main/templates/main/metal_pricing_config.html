Metal Type Category Pricing Status Price Formula
Gold Necklaces, Bangles, Rings, Keyrings, etc.Fixed Jyala (weight_tola × gold_rate) + रु {{ config.gold_fixed_jyala }} Standard (weight_tola × gold_rate) + diamond_value + stone_value + jarti_value + calculated_jyala
Silver Keyrings, Chains, Bracelets, etc.Fixed Jyala (weight_tola × silver_rate) + रु {{ config.silver_fixed_jyala }} Standard (weight_tola × silver_rate) + stone_value + jarti_value + calculated_jyala
Formula Components:
  • weight_tola: Item weight converted to tola (1 tola = 11.664 grams)
  • gold_rate / silver_rate: Today's metal rate per tola from Daily Rates
  • Fixed Jyala: Flat amount added to price (when enabled)
  • Calculated Jyala: Weight-based jyala (Gold: 1000/g, Silver: 3500/g when disabled)
  • Jarti Value: Labor cost (12% of gold weight for gold items)
  • Diamond/Stone Value: Based on weight and per-carat price
How This Works
  • Scope: These rules apply to ALL items of the selected metal type, regardless of category (Necklaces, Keyrings, Bangles, Chains, etc.)
  • Gold Enabled: When enabled, ALL gold items use fixed jyala instead of calculated jyala
  • Silver Enabled: When enabled, ALL silver items use fixed jyala instead of calculated jyala
  • Disabled: When disabled, items use the standard pricing formula with calculated jyala
  • Applies To: Both customer-facing product pages and admin price calculator
  • Daily Rates Impact: Metal rates from "Daily Rates" section are used for calculations
