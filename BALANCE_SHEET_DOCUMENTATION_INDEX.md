## ğŸ’¡ Key Takeaways

### The Formula
```
ASSETS = LIABILITIES + EQUITY
(Must always balance!)
```

### The Components
- **Assets**: What you own (debtors, orders, sales)
- **Liabilities**: What you owe (creditors, salaries, expenses)
- **Equity**: What's yours (assets minus liabilities)

### The Interpretation
- **Growing Equity** = Business is improving
- **Shrinking Equity** = Business is declining
- **Negative Equity** = Business is in trouble

### The Use
- Check financial health
- Make investment decisions
- Plan business growth
- Secure loans/financing
